About Me

My Story

I’m Tanuj Ashok Muley, based in the Greater Toronto Area. I started my career in sales back in 2014, moved to Canada, and grew into a collections leadership role where I combine people management with process improvement and automation. I love turning messy, manual work into clean, repeatable workflows.

Sales & Collections Automation CRM Training

Professional Snapshot

  • 10+ years across B2B sales, account management, and collections.
  • Manager (Collections): coaching teams, building scorecards, and improving cure tools.
  • Hands-on with automation (Power Automate, Outlook/VBA, CRM workflows).
  • Built pilot AI workshops to upskill teammates in practical, safe AI usage.
  • Wrote hundreds of BRDs as SME for an internal CRM modernization effort.
  • Helped design/validate test cases and streamline change requests in JIRA.
  • Obsessed with measurable ROI: hours saved, better CX, cleaner data.
  • Now shaping an AI training practice focused on real-world workflows.
